Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data really private?

Yes. ClearDeskAI uses Ollama to run AI models entirely on your machine. Your files are never uploaded, sent to a server, or processed in the cloud. Everything happens locally.

What are the system requirements?

8GB RAM minimum (for the AI model). Works on Windows 10+, macOS 12+, and Linux. About 3GB of disk space for the app and AI model.

Will it move my files without asking?

Never. Every change is previewed first. You approve each move individually or in bulk. And every operation can be undone with one click.

Why one-time payment instead of subscription?

Because the AI runs on your machine, there's no ongoing server cost for us. You pay once, you own it forever. Major version upgrades are available at a discounted price.
